Welcome to my personal webpage! I'm a physics graduate student with interests in software engineering, scientific computing, and data science.

I spent four years as a Seismic Data Analyst in the US Air Force, where I learned how to handle sensitive data in a professional environment. In the course of my work, I became proficient in Python and Java programming to facilitate data processing. After completing my enlistment, I studied Physics and Computer Science at California State University, Long Beach. I learned to blend both disciplines by simulating physical systems in Python and MATLAB.

I'm currently working on my Master's in Computational Physics. I'm looking for summer internships with the goal of transitioning to a full-time software engineer role when I earn my degree next year.
